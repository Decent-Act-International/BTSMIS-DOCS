                <strong>Add Factory:</strong>
                <br />
                <br />
                <p>
                    The Factory module serves as a centralized database for managing information and documents related
                    to various factories or manufacturing facilities. Each factory is represented by a unique profile
                    that contains essential details and associated documents. Here are some key features and
                    functionalities of this module:
                    <br />
                    <br />
                    <strong>Factory Profiles:</strong> Each factory has a dedicated profile that includes information
                    such as the factory's name, location, contact details, production capacity, operational status, and
                    other relevant information.
                    <br />
                    <br />
                    <strong>Document Management:</strong> The module allows the factory profile to be associated with
                    various documents, such as production reports, quality control records, safety protocols, equipment
                    manuals, maintenance logs, and other important documents specific to the factory.
                    <br />
                    <br />
                    <strong>Document Versioning:</strong> To maintain data integrity, the app may include version
                    control features to track changes made to documents associated with a factory over time.
                    <br />
                    <br />
                    <strong>Access Control:</strong> Access to factory profiles and associated documents can be
                    controlled based on user roles and permissions. This ensures that only authorized personnel, such as
                    factory managers and supervisors, can view and manage sensitive factory-related information.
                </p>

            <section id="factory_list">
                <strong>Factory List:</strong>
                <br />
                <br />
                <p>
                    The Factory List module provides a user-friendly interface that displays a comprehensive list of all
                    the factories or manufacturing facilities managed within the document management app. It serves as a
                    navigational tool to access individual factory profiles and their associated documents efficiently.
                    Here are some key aspects of this module:
                    <br />
                    <br />
                    <strong>List View:</strong> The module presents a tabular or grid-based view of all factories,
                    showing essential details such as factory names, locations, and contact information.
                    <br />
                    <br />
                    <strong>Search and Filters:</strong> Users can search for specific factories based on various
                    criteria, such as factory name, location, production capacity, or any custom fields defined for
                    factory profiles.
                    <br />
                    <br />
                    <strong>Quick Access:</strong> Clicking on a factory's name or profile entry in the list leads to
                    the detailed profile of that factory within the Factory module.
                    <br />
                    <br />
                    <strong>Sorting:</strong> The list may support sorting options, allowing users to arrange factories
                    alphabetically, by location, or other relevant attributes.
                    <br />
                    <br />
                    <strong>Integration with Workers Module:</strong> The Factory List module is closely integrated with
                    the Factory module. Clicking on a factory in the list provides immediate access to its detailed
                    profile and associated documents.
                    <br />
                    <br />
                    By combining the Factory and Factory List modules, the document management app provides an efficient
                    way to manage and access documents related to various factories or manufacturing facilities. It
                    streamlines the process of handling factory-related information, improves document retrieval, and
                    ensures data security and privacy. This setup also enhances the overall organization and management
                    of important records for each factory within the system.
                </p>
