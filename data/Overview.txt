          <h1><strong><strong>Detailed Overview</strong></strong></h1>
          <p>The Bangladesh Tanneries Sector Management Information System Web App is a comprehensive solution designed to streamline and enhance the management of tanneries in Bangladesh. This application facilitates better monitoring, inventory control, production management, and environmental compliance tracking within the tanneries sector.</p>
          <p><strong>Purpose and Scope:</strong> The purpose of this documentation is to provide a comprehensive guide to understanding and effectively using the Tanneries Sector Management Information System Web App. It covers various aspects, including installation, setup, features, user guide, troubleshooting, security measures, and support information.</p>
          <p><strong>Overview of the Bangladesh Tanneries Sector:</strong> This section provides a brief overview of the tanneries industry in Bangladesh, highlighting its significance and challenges. It sets the context for the need for an efficient Management Information System.</p>
          <p><strong>Goals of the Management Information System (MIS) Web App:</strong> This section outlines the key objectives and goals of the MIS Web App, emphasizing its role in improving operational efficiency, compliance, and decision-making within the tanneries sector.</p>
          <p><strong>Intended Audience:</strong> This documentation is intended for tannery owners, managers, staff, and other stakeholders involved in the tanneries sector. It caters to individuals with varying levels of technical expertise and aims to facilitate their understanding and use of the MIS Web App.</p>
          <p>The documentation includes detailed instructions, screenshots, and explanations of the various features and functionalities of the MIS Web App. It guides users through the process of setting up the app, managing tannery information, handling inventory, tracking production, ensuring environmental compliance, and generating financial reports.</p>
          <p>The 'Troubleshooting' section addresses common issues that users may encounter and provides solutions to resolve them. Additionally, the 'Security and Privacy' section outlines the measures taken to protect sensitive data and maintain user privacy.</p>
          <p>For additional assistance and support, users can refer to the 'Support and Training' section, which includes contact information for technical support and links to training resources and user manuals.</p>
          <p>The 'Maintenance and Updates' section explains how to keep the app up-to-date and provides a version history to track changes and improvements over time.</p>
